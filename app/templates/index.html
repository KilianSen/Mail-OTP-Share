Mail-OTP-Share

Securely share one-time passwords from your inbox with trusted family members or colleagues.

Secure

Credentials are encrypted at rest. OTPs are forwarded, not stored.

Time-limited

Shares expire automatically (default 1 hour). Full control over who gets what and for how long.

Email-first

Works entirely over email. Send commands like SHARE REQUEST, APPROVE, or DECLINE.

Sign In with OpenID Connect
Email Commands

Send an email to the system address with one of these subjects:

  • SHARE REQUEST user@example.com — Request OTP sharing from that user
  • APPROVE <request-id> — Approve an incoming share request
  • DECLINE <request-id> — Decline an incoming share request
  • STOP <request-id> — Stop an active share
